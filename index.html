Building a modern software developer, idea woman, product manager, and people nerd: Rachel Ferrall

Born and raised in the Midwest but with itchy feet and a penchant for travel. It started in high school in the northern suburbs of Minneapolis, took me to the University of Minnesota - Morris where I majored in French, Liberal Arts for the Human Services and minored in International Political Science. From the start, I have been fascinated by how and why people organize and act, how language works, and culturally how we connect.

These traits have led me to study abroad, to try my hand at a number of different careers and have fueled my work in hospitality, sales, teaching and now software development. Click the pictures to learn more about my journey.
